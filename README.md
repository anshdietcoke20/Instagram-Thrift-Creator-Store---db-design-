<h1> Instagram Thrift Creator Store - DB Design </h1>
<p> I have designed the ER diagram for the database of a small Instagram business.</p>

<h3>Highlights</h3>
<p> - Categories of the items: thrifted or handmade? </p>
<p> - Product details: colour, size, availability, condition, which category they fall in.</p>
<p> - MUltiple sizes, clear availability status, price, condition and colour of the product will be mentioned ensuring transparency. </p>
<p> - Payment and delivery can be tracked effectively </p>
<p> - Separate sections like customers, orders, products, payments and delivery.</p>
<p> - Such separate sections and correct relationship between them is essential for a clean db design and makes data querying easier.</p>
